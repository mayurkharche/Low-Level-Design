Book My Show Requirements.

1. It should be able to list the cities where affiliate cinemas are located.
2. Each cinema can have multiple Audis and each Audi can run one movie show at a time.
3. Each movie will have mutiple shows.
4. Customers should be able to search movies by their title, language, genre, release date and city name.
5. Once a customer selects a movie, the service should display the cinemas running that movie and its available shows.
6. The customer should be able to select a show at a particular cinema and book their tickets.
7. The service should show the customer, seating arrangement of the cinema hall. The customer should be able to select multiple seats according to their preference.
8. The customer should be able to distinguish between available seats and booked ones.
