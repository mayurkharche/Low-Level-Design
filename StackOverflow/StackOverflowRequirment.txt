Stack Overflow Requirements

1. Any non member (guest) can search and view questions.
2. Only members should be able to post a question.
3. Members should be able to add an answer.
4. Members can add comments to any question and answer.
5. A member can upvote a question, answer or comment.
6. Members can flag a question, answer or comment, for serious problems and moderators attention.
7. Members will earn badges.
8. Members can vote to delete or close any questions.
9. Moderators can close a question or undelete an already deleted question.
